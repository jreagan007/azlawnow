Newsroom: [Station name]

Your newsroom carried the breaking on Sonja Celius on April 25 at 27th Avenue and Thomas Road. The follow-on the federal record supports:

Between June 2024 (when BNSF and the City of Phoenix completed the two-quadrant gate retrofit) and the April 25, 2026 Sonja Celius strike, BNSF Railway Company filed three Form 6180.57 accident reports at the twin Thomas Road crossing fifty feet north. All three are post-gate. Two filings (June 15, 2025 and August 7, 2025) carry the same BNSF narrative phrase: "AUTO PRECEDED GATES." The third (December 15, 2025) reads in part: "THE AUTO CAME UP AGAINST THE DIRECTION OF TRAFFIC FLOW NOT HAVING GATES." One passenger was injured in that incident.

The mitigations specified by 49 CFR Part 222 (channelizing medians, four-quadrant gates, supplementary safety measures) were not included in the June 2024 scope. The FRA inventory revised July 18, 2025 confirms no channelization devices or four-quadrant gates at either 27th Avenue or Thomas Road.
