Maricopa, we're officially here.

AZ Law Now opened its new office at 21300 N John Wayne Pkwy #109-B, in the Maricopa Professional Village. Thanks to the Greater Maricopa Business Alliance for hosting the ribbon-cutting. And to Roots Eatery for the food, which was the real star of the day.

Here's why we opened here.

## The Commute Problem

For years, if you got hurt in a crash on SR-347 or I-10 near Maricopa, your options were limited. Drive to Phoenix. Drive to Chandler. Or call a firm that didn't know the roads you drive every day.

That never made sense to us. Maricopa is one of the fastest-growing cities in Arizona. The population has roughly doubled in the last decade. SR-347 carries tens of thousands of commuters between Maricopa and the Valley every morning. When things go wrong on that corridor, residents shouldn't have to spend an extra hour driving just to find legal representation.

So we opened here.

## What We Handle Locally

The practice is dedicated to injury law. That's it. If a crash, a fall, or someone's negligence caused real harm, we handle the case.

The corridors we know best are the ones Maricopa residents drive. [SR-347 between Maricopa and the I-10 interchange](/investigations/sr-347-crash-corridor/) is one of the most consistent problem areas in the state for serious crashes, and we've written about why the geometry and traffic volume make it that way. The I-10 stretch from Casa Grande up through Chandler sees heavy truck traffic, and commercial vehicle claims follow a different playbook than standard car crash claims.

We're building the Maricopa office to handle both, along with everything else that falls under Arizona injury law: premises liability, daycare and nursing home negligence, wrongful death, and abuse cases.

## Part of the Community

Joining the Greater Maricopa Business Alliance wasn't a marketing move. It was the point.

We're not a Phoenix billboard firm with a phone number and a PO box in Maricopa. The office is here. The staff is here. The cases come from here. And when the Alliance hosts events, sponsors community initiatives, or connects local businesses, we show up.

If you live in Maricopa, Casa Grande, Coolidge, or anywhere along the SR-347 corridor, the office is open. Walk in. Call. Or send a note through [the contact page](/contact/). We'll meet you where you're.
