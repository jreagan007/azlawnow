## What it means

Legal responsibility of bars, restaurants, and other alcohol vendors for injuries caused by patrons they served while obviously intoxicated.

## Arizona context

Arizona's dram shop statute is ARS 4-311. A licensee can be liable if they served alcohol to an obviously intoxicated person or a person under 21, and the consumption was a proximate cause of the injury.
