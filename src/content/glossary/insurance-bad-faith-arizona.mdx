## What it means

Arizona allows policyholders to sue their own insurer for unreasonable handling of a claim. The standard requires showing the insurer acted unreasonably and knew or recklessly disregarded the unreasonableness.

## Arizona context

Established in Noble v. National American Life Insurance Co., 128 Ariz. 188 (1981). Arizona is a leading state for first-party bad faith law.
