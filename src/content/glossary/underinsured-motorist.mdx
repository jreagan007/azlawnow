## What it means

Optional auto insurance that pays the gap when an at-fault driver's liability limits are too low to cover your damages. Critical when injuries exceed the at-fault driver's coverage.

## Arizona context

Arizona's minimum liability limits (25/50/15) often aren't enough to cover serious injuries. UIM coverage from your own policy fills the gap up to your UIM limits.
