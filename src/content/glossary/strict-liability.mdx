## What it means

Legal responsibility for damages regardless of fault or intent. The plaintiff doesn't have to prove negligence, only that the defendant's action caused the harm.

## Arizona context

Arizona applies strict liability to dog bites (ARS 11-1025), product defects, and abnormally dangerous activities. The owner of a dog is liable for bites regardless of the dog's history.
