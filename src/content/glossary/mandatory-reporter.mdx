## What it means

A person required by law to report suspected abuse, neglect, or exploitation of children or vulnerable adults. Includes teachers, healthcare workers, social workers, and clergy.

## Arizona context

Arizona's mandatory reporting laws are in ARS 13-3620 (children) and ARS 46-454 (vulnerable adults). Failure to report is a class 1 misdemeanor and can rise to a class 6 felony.
