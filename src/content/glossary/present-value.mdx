## What it means

The current worth of a future sum of money, calculated by discounting future amounts to account for the time value of money. Used to value future damages in personal injury cases.

## Arizona context

Arizona requires future damages (medical, earning capacity) to be reduced to present value before being awarded. Economists typically testify about appropriate discount rates.
