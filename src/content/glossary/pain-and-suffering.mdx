## What it means

Compensation for the physical pain and emotional distress caused by an injury. Both past pain and reasonably anticipated future pain are compensable.

## Arizona context

Arizona juries determine pain and suffering damages based on the evidence. There is no statutory formula. The damages are not capped in most cases.
