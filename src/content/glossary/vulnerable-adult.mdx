## What it means

Under Arizona law, a person 18 or older who is unable to protect themselves from abuse, neglect, or exploitation due to a physical or mental impairment.

## Arizona context

Defined in ARS 46-451. The vulnerable adult designation triggers mandatory reporting duties and enhanced penalties for abuse.
