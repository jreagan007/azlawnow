## What it means

No-fault auto insurance coverage that pays medical bills, lost wages, and related expenses regardless of fault. Required in some states but not Arizona.

## Arizona context

Arizona is NOT a no-fault state. PIP is not required and not commonly sold. Arizona drivers rely on liability, UM/UIM, and Med Pay coverage.
