## What it means

Subjective losses that don't have a clear dollar value. Includes pain and suffering, mental anguish, loss of enjoyment of life, disfigurement, and loss of consortium.

## Arizona context

Arizona has no cap on non-economic damages in most personal injury cases. The state constitution prohibits caps on damages for death or injury (Arizona Constitution, Article II, Section 31).
