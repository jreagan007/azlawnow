## What it means

The legal deadline for filing a lawsuit. Once the deadline passes, the right to sue is generally lost forever, regardless of the merits of the claim.

## Arizona context

Arizona's statute of limitations for personal injury is two years from the date of injury (ARS 12-542). Different deadlines apply to claims against government entities (180 days notice, 1 year to sue) and minors (clock starts at age 18).
