## What it means

Latin for 'let the master answer.' The legal doctrine that holds employers responsible for the negligent acts of employees performed within the scope of their job.

## Arizona context

Arizona applies respondeat superior in commercial vehicle cases. Trucking companies can be held liable for crashes caused by their drivers, even if the company itself wasn't negligent.
