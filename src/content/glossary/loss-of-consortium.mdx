## What it means

Damages for the loss of companionship, affection, sexual relations, and household services suffered by the spouse of an injured person.

## Arizona context

Arizona recognizes loss of consortium claims for spouses. The claim is derivative of the injured spouse's underlying claim and may be reduced by the injured spouse's comparative fault.
