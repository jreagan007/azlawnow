## What it means

Damages for the reduction in a person's ability to earn money in the future, separate from actual lost wages already incurred.

## Arizona context

Arizona allows recovery for both past lost wages and future loss of earning capacity. Future earnings are typically reduced to present value using expert economic testimony.
