## What it means

A common law rule that prevents defendants from reducing damages by the amount of money the plaintiff received from independent sources (insurance, government benefits, etc.).

## Arizona context

Arizona retains the collateral source rule with some limitations. Generally, evidence of insurance payments or other collateral sources is not admissible to reduce damages.
