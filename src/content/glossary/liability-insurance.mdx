## What it means

Insurance that covers damages an insured person becomes legally responsible for paying to another person. The core protection in any auto, business, or homeowner's policy.

## Arizona context

Arizona requires drivers to carry minimum liability coverage of $25,000 per person, $50,000 per accident, and $15,000 property damage (25/50/15) under ARS 28-4135.
