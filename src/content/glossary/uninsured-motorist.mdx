## What it means

An optional auto insurance coverage that pays for your injuries if you're hit by a driver who has no insurance. Required to be offered in Arizona but can be rejected in writing.

## Arizona context

Arizona requires insurers to offer UM coverage, but drivers can reject it in writing. Arizona's mandatory liability minimums are 25/50/15, but many drivers carry only the minimum or drive uninsured.
