## What it means

The right of an insurance company that paid for medical care to recover those payments from the at-fault party or their insurance. Health insurers often have subrogation rights.

## Arizona context

Arizona recognizes subrogation rights for health insurers, workers' compensation carriers, and Medicare. Settlement negotiations often involve resolving these liens.
