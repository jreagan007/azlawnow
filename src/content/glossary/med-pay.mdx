## What it means

Optional auto insurance coverage that pays medical bills for you and your passengers regardless of who caused the crash. Typically $1,000 to $10,000 limits.

## Arizona context

Arizona doesn't require Med Pay. It's optional add-on coverage. It can be useful for covering immediate medical expenses while liability claims are pending.
