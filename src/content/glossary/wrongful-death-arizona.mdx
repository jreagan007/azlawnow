## What it means

A civil action brought by certain family members when a person dies due to another's wrongful conduct. Damages compensate the survivors for their losses.

## Arizona context

Arizona's wrongful death statutes are ARS 12-611 through 12-613. Surviving spouses, children, parents, and personal representatives can bring claims. There is no cap on damages.
