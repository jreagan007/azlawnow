## What it means

The level of care a reasonably prudent person or professional would exercise in the same situation. The benchmark used to judge whether a defendant breached their duty.

## Arizona context

For medical professionals in Arizona, the standard of care is what a reasonably competent practitioner in the same specialty would do under similar circumstances. Expert testimony is required.
